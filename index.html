DIY-Portfolio

A free and simple website template for makers who aren't afraid of a little html.

Applying to college? Or just need a simple website to showcase your work?

DIY-Portfolio is a free, downloadable website template that you can edit to create your own

personal project portfolio. It's open-source, so you can keep all your changes.

Why not just use Tumblr, or Wordpress?

.

That's a good question, and I think that you should absolutely use Tumblr or Wordpress if

you prefer how they look. They are also easier to use out of the box, no question.

.

But if you want a little more control, hate editing themes, or are just curious

about learning some HTML, then DIY-Portfolio might be perfect for you.

How does it work?

.

You can download the template from this Github link. The website is written

in HTML and CSS, and I tried to keep it as simple as possible. Once you have downloaded the

template, that's it. There are no more instructions. Open up the files, and figure it out.

.

The code is optimized for readibility, and there are lots of comments to help you out.

As you get stronger at HTML, you will notice little things here and there that could be improved.

This is a good thing.

Mess around.

.

Don't worry about breaking things. You can always undo the mistake.

Start by changing words you recognize, then see what happens. Google anything

you don't understand.

Why did I make this?

.

I made DIY-Portfolio as a template for my own website. Check it out as an example.

.

Why make another website template when there are already hundreds of options for things like this?

.

Good question. I don't have an answer.

I learned to code by hacking other people's code, hopefully you can do the same.
